Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, select "Create Group", add a group name, and invite members by their email addresses.

How do I split an expense?

When adding an expense, you can split it equally among all group members, by percentage, or by custom amounts.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use Supabase for secure authentication and data storage. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Can I export my expense data?

Currently, you can view all your expense history in the app. Data export functionality is coming soon!

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data. This action cannot be undone.
